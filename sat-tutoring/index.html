In New York, Your SAT & ACT Score Sets the Stage.

Our Method

How Whetstone Students Actually Improve

Repetition without understanding doesn't move scores. We build mastery — then test it relentlessly under real conditions.

1

SAT & ACT Diagnostic

Every student takes a full-length SAT and ACT diagnostic. We identify error patterns by question type and recommend the test where you have the largest natural edge.

2

Active Recall & the Feynman Method

We don't re-read notes or re-watch videos. Students learn by retrieving — and by explaining concepts back in their own words until there are no gaps. Spaced repetition locks it in.

3

Heavy Testing Under Real Conditions

Students take timed full-length practice tests throughout the program — not at the end. Every test is debriefed question by question to convert errors into mastered concepts.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

When should my student start tutoring? +
Most students begin in the fall or winter of their junior year to prepare for the March or May exams. We recommend starting with a diagnostic as early as sophomore summer to allow the most time for improvement.
Should my child take the SAT or the ACT? +
Both tests are accepted equally by every major college and university. The right choice depends on the student's strengths. We administer a diagnostic for both and recommend the test where the student has a larger natural advantage.
How is the Digital SAT different from the old paper test? +
The Digital SAT (launched March 2024) is shorter (2 hours 14 minutes vs. 3 hours), adaptive by module, and delivered on a computer or tablet. The adaptive format means early performance heavily influences your final score. Our tutors are trained specifically on Digital SAT strategy and official College Board practice materials.
How are Whetstone instructors selected? +
Our instructors are exclusively recruited from Ivy League and top-10 universities. Each must score in the 99th percentile and undergo a rigorous pedagogical training process before working with students.
How does the score guarantee work? +
Each program tier includes a specific score guarantee. If your student completes the program in good standing — attending sessions, completing assigned practice, and sitting for a real exam — and does not hit the target, we extend tutoring at no additional charge until they do. Details are outlined in the enrollment agreement.
