Why Whetstone

What Makes Whetstone Different

Our founders built one of the largest philosophy and education nonprofits in the country. That shaped our belief that admissions success should grow out of strong character – not the other way around.

  • 01

    Mentorship, Not Just Admissions

    Our aim is not just to guide applications, but to become trusted mentors and friends during some of the most formative years of life. That is why we work with a deliberately small number of students.

  • 02

    Disciplined Execution

    Strong strategy means little without follow-through. We use science-backed systems to help students build the habits, focus, and accountability needed to achieve ambitious goals.

  • 03

    Love of Wisdom

    We see admissions as one important moment in a much larger journey. Through the Great Books, our students develop a curiosity and a genuine love of learning often overlooked by modern high schools.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Our team is drawn from the world's most rigorous academic institutions — Harvard, Oxford, MIT, Yale, and Princeton. Each advisor has personally navigated the admissions process at the highest level, and works with a deliberately small number of students per cycle to ensure genuine individualization.

The spring of junior year is ideal — when testing is largely set, extracurriculars are in their final shape, and there's time to develop essay ideas without pressure. For students targeting extracurricular development or the Capstone program, sophomore year is better. We also work with seniors and late starters.

Our philosophy: practice doesn't make perfect — perfect practice does. Every session is highly intentional. We begin with an honest profile assessment, build a coherent strategy across essays, activities, and testing, and track progress at every milestone.

We work with high school students applying to undergraduate programs at selective universities, primarily from New York City (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Westchester) and internationally. We also support graduate applicants through our Graduate Admissions program.

We begin with a free consultation to understand your goals, timeline, and profile. We then build a customized engagement — which may include admissions counseling, essay coaching, test prep, or extracurricular mentorship depending on what your application needs most.

No admissions service can guarantee acceptance — and you should be skeptical of any that claim otherwise. We can guarantee that you submit the most polished and strategic application you are capable of. Our SAT programs include score guarantees. See our results →
