FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Our team is made up of experienced admissions consultants, tutors, and test prep specialists who understand what it takes to succeed in today's competitive admissions landscape. Each counselor is dedicated to providing precise feedback, structured guidance, and personalized strategies to help students perform at their highest potential. Whether working on test prep, essays, or interviews, our counselors ensure every effort is intentional and aligned with long-term success.

The earlier you begin, the stronger your application strategy will be. We recommend starting 6–12 months before application deadlines so there's enough time for intentional preparation—whether that means building a testing plan, developing essays, or strengthening extracurricular profiles. Early planning allows us to structure each step with precision, giving students the confidence and clarity to perform at their best when it matters most.

Our philosophy is simple: practice doesn't make perfect—perfect practice does. Every session, whether tutoring, test preparation, or application coaching, is designed to be highly intentional. We focus on efficiency, accuracy, and results so that students maximize every effort.

We support high school, undergraduate, and graduate students preparing for competitive admissions. Our team also works with international students applying to U.S. schools and programs.

We begin with a consultation to learn about your goals and challenges. Then we create a customized plan that includes tutoring, test prep strategies, essay coaching, and admissions guidance tailored to your needs.

No admissions service can guarantee acceptance. However, we can ensure that you submit the most polished and competitive application possible, supported by strong test performance and compelling essays.
