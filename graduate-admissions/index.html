Our Graduate Admissions Process

A Rigorous, Three-Stage Method

We treat each graduate application as an extended intellectual argument — one that must be coherent, compelling, and precisely tailored to each program's academic culture.

1

Profile Assessment & School Selection

We begin with a rigorous assessment of your academic trajectory, professional experience, research interests, and long-term goals. From there, we build a strategic school list that matches your intellectual profile to the right programs — whether you are targeting Harvard Business School, Yale Law, or a top-ranked PhD program in your field.

2

Statement of Purpose & Personal Statement

Your statement of purpose is the single most scrutinised document in your graduate application. We work 1:1 with you through every draft, sharpening your scholarly narrative, clarifying your research agenda, and ensuring that every sentence advances your intellectual profile with precision. For law and MBA applicants, we bring the same discipline to personal statements and optional essays.

3

Interview Preparation & Final Review

Many competitive graduate programs — particularly MBA programmes and MD/PhD tracks — include rigorous interviews. We conduct multiple mock interviews, review every component of your submitted application for consistency, and ensure your academic and professional narrative reads as a single, authoritative whole. You will walk into your interview prepared, confident, and precise.
