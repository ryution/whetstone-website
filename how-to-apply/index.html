How to Apply

Applications for the Summer 2026 Academy Fellowship are now open and close May 15, 2026. The application consists of two short essays, an official transcript, and a brief interview conducted by members of our admissions committee.

We use a holistic review process. Academic achievement matters, but we are equally interested in how you think and what you care about. There is no ideal background or profile.

Applications Now Open

Applications are open through May 15, 2026. We encourage interested students to apply early, as space is limited.

Eligibility

In order to apply to the Academy Fellowship, applicants must fulfill all of the following requirements:

Grade LevelRising juniors, rising seniors, and incoming college freshmen
AgeAt least 16 years old by June 20, 2026 and may not turn 19 before July 31, 2026
LocationMust be able to commute to Midtown Manhattan for weekly seminars
LanguageEnglish fluency sufficient for extended Socratic seminar participation
First-timePriority given to first-time Academy Fellowship applicants

Required Components

All applicants must submit the following:

  • Two short essays (prompts provided in application)
  • Official high school transcript
  • Brief interview with admissions committee (by invitation)

No letters of recommendation are required. No application fee.

Admissions Questions

For questions about the application process, eligibility, or financial aid, contact our Head of Admissions, Harrison Orlando, at fellowship@nyphilosophy.org.
