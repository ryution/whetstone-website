The NYC Standard

In New York, a Good Score Isn't Enough.

Students at Manhattan's top independent schools and Westchester's most competitive districts aren't competing against the national average — they're competing against each other. At Dalton, Collegiate, Spence, and Horace Mann, a 1400 is the floor, not the ceiling.

Generic test prep centers weren't designed for this environment. They run students through the same course regardless of baseline, assign rotating instructors who don't know the student, and offer no real accountability when scores plateau.

Whetstone was built differently. Every engagement begins with a full diagnostic, pairs the student with a single dedicated tutor for the duration, and doesn't end until the target score is reached — or we extend the program at no additional cost.

Our Method

Perfect Practice Makes Perfect

Most students practice ineffectively — repetition without correction. We fix that from session one.

1

Full Diagnostic

A complete practice test followed by detailed error analysis reveals exactly where points are being lost — by question type, timing, and error pattern.

2

Custom Study Plan

Your dedicated tutor builds a plan targeting the highest-leverage skills — using active recall and spaced repetition, not volume drilling.

3

Accountable Progress

Weekly 1:1 sessions, homework review, and monthly score projections keep the student on pace. Parents receive regular updates throughout.
