Our Method

How Whetstone Helps Students Build Standout Applications

A deliberate, five-part process for building the kind of application that admissions officers at selective universities remember.

Book a Consultation

Step One

1

Discover Your Admissions Roadmap.

The first step is understanding who you are as an applicant — honestly. We review your transcript, testing history, extracurricular record, and ambitions. We set a realistic target school list and build a 12-month timeline that accounts for testing retakes, essay drafts, and every application deadline.

Every student leaves this stage knowing exactly what they need to do and when.

Learn about Admissions Counseling →

Step Two

2

Build a Distinctive Extracurricular Profile.

The most selective universities don't admit lists of clubs — they admit students with a demonstrated intellectual identity. We help students identify, develop, and position their extracurricular activities so they reinforce the central narrative of the application.

For students earlier in the process, the Whetstone Capstone Accelerator guides them in building an original project — something real, high-impact, and authentically theirs.

Learn about Extracurricular Mentorship →

Step Three

3

Write Essays That Matter.

Your transcript and scores tell admissions officers what you're capable of. Your essays tell them who you are. We work with Princeton's writing specialists to develop a personal statement that is surprising, specific, and memorable — and supplementals that add depth rather than repeat what's already in the application.

We don't produce generic essays. We produce essays that only you could have written.

Learn about Essay Coaching →

Step Four

4

Raise Your Scores With Purpose.

We don't recommend testing strategy in the abstract — we build a plan based on your baseline scores, the score requirements of your target schools, and the time you realistically have available.

Our SAT tutors scored in the 99th percentile and work with students in person in NYC and online worldwide. Score guarantee included.

Learn about SAT / ACT Prep →

Step Five

5

Develop Intellectual Depth.

The most compelling applicants don't just describe their achievements — they demonstrate genuine intellectual curiosity. Through Whetstone's Great Books curriculum and guided reading, students develop the vocabulary, perspective, and ideas that elevate their essays and interviews from competent to remarkable.

This strand of our work isn't for every student — but for the right one, it transforms an application.
