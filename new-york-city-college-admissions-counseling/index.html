Why NYC Is Different

New York City Is the Most Competitive Market in the Country.

Students in New York City's top independent schools aren't competing against the national average — they're competing against each other. At Dalton, Collegiate, Spence, Trinity, Horace Mann, and Stuyvesant, the baseline is already exceptional. A strong GPA and a 1400 SAT won't distinguish you at schools where everyone has the same.

Whetstone was built for this environment — where the difference between admission and rejection is the quality of the narrative, not the credentials.
