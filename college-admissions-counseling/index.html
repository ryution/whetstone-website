Why It Matters

Most Counselors Tell You What You Want to Hear.

School counselors at even the most prestigious high schools manage 80 to 300 students at a time. Independent consultants often recycle templates and charge premium fees for generic advice. The result: applications that look like every other application from your school.

Whetstone works with a deliberately small number of students per season. Your advisor knows your application in full — your essays, your activities, your story — and builds every piece of it alongside you.
