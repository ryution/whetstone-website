Our Program

The Academy Fellowship emphasizes an open, rigorous, and collaborative approach to learning. The program is designed around the Socratic method: close reading, sustained dialogue, and the willingness to follow an argument wherever it leads.

Seminar Structure

Fellows meet one to two times per week in Midtown Manhattan for extended seminars of two to three hours. Between sessions, fellows are expected to complete 10–15 hours of independent reading.

Seminars are small by design to ensure that every voice is heard and every idea genuinely tested. Faculty do not lecture, but guide discussion with questions.
