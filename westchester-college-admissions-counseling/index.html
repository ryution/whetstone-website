Westchester's High-Stakes Admissions Environment

Westchester's Top Districts Send Students to the Nation's Most Selective Universities.

Scarsdale, Bronxville, Chappaqua, and Larchmont-Mamaroneck are among the highest-performing school districts in the country. Their students consistently apply to Ivy League and Top 15 universities — and increasingly, they compete with students from Manhattan's independent schools for the same spots.

In this environment, a strong academic record is expected. What distinguishes an application is strategy, narrative, and the quality of execution across essays, activities, and testing.
