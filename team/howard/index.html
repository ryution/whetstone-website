Howard

Capstone Mentor

Howard has been a lawyer in New York specializing in litigation for more than thirty years. He has worked at prestigious firms in the city, and is recognized as a polished and forceful writer and advocate.

After graduating summa cum laude in History from Yale University, Howard went on to pursue his Juris Doctor at Harvard Law School. Howard has a logical and incisive mind that enables him to diagnose any academic problem, and to suggest practical solutions for improvement.

In his private practice, Howard notably litigated a high-profile censorship case involving Meta and also successfully lobbied Gov. Kathy Hochul to sign protective legislation for the nation's oldest afterschool program. At Whetstone, Howard enjoys mentoring the next generation of ambitious and talented students.
