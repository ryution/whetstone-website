Eric Newman

Elite Tutor

After graduating from MIT with a degree in physics, Eric worked at Harvard, MIT, and Peking University as a researcher specializing in string theory and black holes.

Eric's teaching philosophy emphasizes intuitive understanding over rote memorization, tailoring his approach to each student's goals and learning style. With over 10 years of tutoring experience, Eric loves teaching students to understand and appreciate the elegant simplicity underlying math and physics.

Eric is currently working on writing a philosophical novel. He also loves linguistics and playing billiards.
